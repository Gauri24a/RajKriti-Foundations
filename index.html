RajKriti Foundations – Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

We are committed to creating sustainable change through education, healthcare, and social empowerment.

Join Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift underserved communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and opportunities that enable individuals to lead dignified and self-reliant lives.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every individual has equal access to basic needs, opportunities, and the ability to shape their own future.

Who We Are

About Us

RajKriti Foundations is a non-profit organization dedicated to social welfare and community development. Founded on the belief that collective action can drive meaningful and lasting change, we aim to empower individuals and uplift communities through inclusive and sustainable initiatives.

We collaborate closely with local communities to understand their needs and address critical challenges such as education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and social equity.

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Our Projects

Initiatives creating real change on the ground

🍱

Project Annapurna

Nourishing Lives, Preventing Waste

Bridging the gap between surplus food and hunger by redistributing leftover meals from weddings, events, and vendors to underprivileged communities.

🎯 Eradicating hunger & minimizing food waste
🏥

Project Aarogya

Health for All

Improving community health through regular medical camps, health checkups, and blood donation drives in partnership with hospitals and healthcare volunteers.

🎯 Accessible & affordable healthcare
💪

Project Shakti

Empowering Minds, Enabling Futures

Creating equal opportunities through mentorship, skill development workshops, and educational activities — while empowering women toward financial independence.

🎯 Education & women's livelihood
