# RajKriti Foundations 🌱

**Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives**

RajKriti Foundations is a non-profit organization dedicated to social welfare and community development, based in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand. This repository contains the official website of the foundation — built to share our mission, showcase our projects, and connect with volunteers, donors, and well-wishers.

## 🌍 About the Organization

Founded on the belief that collective action can drive meaningful and lasting change, RajKriti Foundations works closely with underserved communities to address critical challenges in education, healthcare, food security, and women's empowerment.

We aim to build a more compassionate and equitable society — one community at a time.

## 🎯 Our Mission

To uplift underserved communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and opportunities that enable individuals to lead dignified and self-reliant lives.

## 👁️ Our Vision

A society where every individual has equal access to basic needs, opportunities, and the ability to shape their own future.

## 🤝 Our Projects

### 🍱 Project Annapurna — Nourishing Lives, Preventing Waste
Bridging the gap between surplus food and hunger by redistributing leftover meals from weddings, events, and vendors to underprivileged communities.

### 🏥 Project Aarogya — Health for All
Improving community health through regular medical camps, health checkups, and blood donation drives in partnership with hospitals and healthcare volunteers.

### 💪 Project Shakti — Empowering Minds, Enabling Futures
Creating equal opportunities through mentorship, skill development workshops, and educational activities — while empowering women toward financial independence.
